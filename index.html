    • About Horror Vacui

    Horror Vacui is an italian post-punk, modern goth/death rock band from Bologna, Italy. The band was formed in 2010 and has 5 current members. They released a few singles on soundcloud before releasing their first album "In Darkness You Will Feel Alright" in 2012. Although they are not the most known band, in the Goth community they are slowly being recognized as one of the "best modern goth rock bands".
